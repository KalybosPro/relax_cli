# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Abusive, insulting, or demeaning comments and actions.
- Publishing other people’s private information without consent.
- Inappropriate or unprofessional language or imagery.
- Disruption of discussions or intentionally derailing conversations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces including GitHub issues, pull requests, commit comments, discussion forums, and any other communication channels for the `relax_cli` project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers through the issue tracker:

- https://github.com/KalybosPro/relax_cli/issues

Reports should include as much detail as possible. All reports will be handled with discretion and care.

## Enforcement Guidelines

If a maintainer determines that a participant has violated this Code of Conduct, a range of responses may be applied, including:

- A private warning from the maintainers.
- Temporary or permanent removal of contribution privileges.
- Blocking or banning from project communication channels.
